Vigilance Section

The Vigilance Section of Delhi Technological University(DTU) works for maintaining probity in public life, provides necessary guidelines for effectively carrying out vigilance administration in all departments of the University through various circulars/directives from CVC and undertakes preventive, punitive and surveillance & detection activities. It handles complaints under public Interest disclosure and investigates into them in an impartial and time bound manner.
